Who We Are

PT. Kurnia Jaya Aditama was founded in December 2024 and operates in the Mining, Trading & General Contracting sectors.

Early experiences and a progressive learning curve have driven the company to continue to grow and develop sustainably. By implementing our core corporate values—Integrity, Commitment, and Care (ICC)—and supported by high-quality strategic resources, we are dedicated to upholding our commitment to provide the best service to customers and become a reliable, quality partner.

PT. Kurnia Jaya Aditama is ready to collaborate with all professional networks and partners across the mining, trading, and general contractor sectors with full responsibility and mutual benefit. Every trust given to us is an honor that we will maintain and implement perfectly to ensure outstanding and satisfactory results for both parties.
